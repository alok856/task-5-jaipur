About Jaipur

Jaipur, also known as the "Pink City" is the capital of Rajasthan, India. The city is known for its rich history, stunning architecture, and vibrant culture. With its grand palaces, colorful markets, and beautiful forts, Jaipur offers a fascinating glimpse into the royal past of India. Jaipur is also known for its Hawa Mahal, Amber Fort, and City Palace, all of which attract tourists from around the world.
